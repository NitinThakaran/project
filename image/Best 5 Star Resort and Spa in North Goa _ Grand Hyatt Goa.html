    Grand Hyatt Goa

    Experience our Luxury Hotel in Goa

    Redefining luxury at the heart of Goa, Grand Hyatt Goa overlooks the visually stunning waters of the Bambolim Bay. This hotel in Goa spreads across 28 acres of colourful, tropical gardens and lush lawns that roll down to the water’s edge of the Bambolim Bay.

    Spa

    Shamana Spa
    Shamana is a Sanskrit word for balance. Shamana Spa at Grand Hyatt Goa is the resort’s temple of wellness that offers a peaceful and serene retreat. With a combination of modern and ancient Indian techniques, the essence of Shamana Spa is to create the perfect balance between well-being and lifestyle for a youthful you. Escape to this luxury spa oasis overlooking the calm water of Bambolim Bay

    Our Hotel

    Adventure Park

    Adventure Park

    Go on an adrenaline rush with our adventure package that includes zip line, wall climbing, rope course, sky cycle and more, starting at INR1350.

    Swimming pool

    Swimming pool

    Experience the grandness in unmatched style and luxury with the outdoor pool that features leisure pool facilities and a kids' pool facing Bambolim Bay.

    The 25-metre indoor lap pool provides a complete lifestyle experience. The separate steam and sauna facilities adjacent to the whirlpools ensure rejuvenation in a style that is grand and all-inclusive.

    The Galleria

    The Galleria

    Experience our shopping space, featuring a combination of premium and quirky brands that range from Jamavar, Mario Miranda,  Freedom Tree, Johns, Speedo and MacV.

    Aquasail Sailing Centre

    Aquasail Sailing Centre

    A great experience awaits you with a top of the line fleet and certified instructors allowing individual and groups to achieve the best in sailing 

    Casino Strike by Big Daddy

    Casino Strike by Big Daddy

    Casino Strike by Big Daddy offers a world-class gaming experience, through a variety of international games including Baccarat, Andar Bahar, BlackJack, Mini Flush, Roulette, Casino War and Slots, with the latest gaming equipment.

    Dining

    With a selection of seven restaurants and bars, you can savour the traditional Indian flavours at Chulha, five world cuisines at The Dining Room or the finest in Italian at The Verandah. Experience the unique ambience of the popular Capiz Bar or Pool Bar and Grill. Catch up for a quick bite and coffee at Bay View Lounge or Confeitaria.
