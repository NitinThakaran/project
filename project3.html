MEETINGS

Grand Hyatt Goa is Goa’s largest and most sophisticated luxury conference and wedding resort, bringing the ultimate event experience to the dynamic meeting and weddings scene, with an overall banquet facility of over 3500 sq m. The versatile function rooms are located across two floors. The elegant Grand Ballroom and six functions rooms are on the ground level with a pre-function area ideally equipped to hold receptions. The lobby level also houses two meeting rooms and two board rooms.
